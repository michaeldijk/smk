Welcome to my store

Established in 1999. Voted “Top best 50 shops” in Ireland, by readers of the IRISH TIMES 2012 & 2013.

Stone Mad Gallery & Stone Mad Emporium sit side by side in the colourful bohemian quarter of Newmans mall, Kinsale, Co. Cork. Entering Stone Mad Gallery is like stepping into a tiny jewellery box, filled with beautiful sparkling adornments & bohemian clothing.

Stone Mad Emporium is just that !! Everything to accessorize your wardrobe and your home . Come and peruse the jewellery landscape that is Stone Mad Gallery and let us help you find your favorite piece of jewellery. Jill x
